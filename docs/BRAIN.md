# Project Brain

## Product Intent
AI for Nurses India is a bilingual (English-Hindi), mobile-first SaaS/LMS web application designed for the Indian nursing ecosystem. It aims to provide AI awareness, literacy, and practical skills while ensuring responsible, safe use in nursing education and clinical settings.

## Target Users
- B.Sc. Nursing Students
- Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing Students
- M.Sc. Nursing Students
- PhD Scholars
- Working Nurses
- Nurse Educators / Faculty
- Administrators & Institutional Coordinators
- Mentors

## Core Value Proposition
"AI literacy, safe AI use, productivity, research, teaching, and career growth for Indian nurses."

## Domain Assumptions & Safety Boundaries
- AI is not a clinician; it does not replace medical judgment.
- No patient-specific diagnosis or prescriptive advice.
- Strict prevention against uploading Personally Identifiable Information (PII) / Protected Health Information (PHI).
- Content aligned with Indian Nursing Council (INC) frameworks (competency-based).
- Human oversight required for AI-generated drafts.

## Implementation Status
- **Phase 0:** Initial documentation setup (In Progress).
- **Current Stack Selection:** Next.js 15+ App Router, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, PostgreSQL (Prisma/Drizzle), Better Auth/Auth.js.

## Reference Standards
- Indian Nursing Council official regulations.
- India DPDP Act 2023.
- WHO ethics & regulatory considerations on AI for health.
- WCAG 2.2 AA.
- OWASP Top 10.
